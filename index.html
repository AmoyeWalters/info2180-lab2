My Journey as an upcoming Software Engineer

October 6, 2024 by Amoye Walters

During high school, I developed a deep love for science subjects. They revealed the intricate details of both simple and complex phenomena that occur in everyday life, and I was always excited to learn something new with each lesson. I also enjoyed technical subjects such as Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical Engineering, finding them both challenging and fun. Despite my enthusiasm for these subjects, I was unsure about my future career path. In Grade 11, I considered becoming a pharmacist, inspired by my passion for science. However, when I applied for a pharmacy program, my application was denied.

Feeling uncertain, I decided to apply for a degree in Software Engineering, a field I knew little about at the time. This decision was influenced by a friend's recommendation, who also applied. Initially, I reached a near breaking point, fearing that my future prospects were dwindling. However, after overcoming a state of denial, I began to see this new direction as a blessing in disguise. I hoped that this unexpected path would ignite my interest and lead me to a fulfilling career.

Reflections on My Ongoing Journey in Software Engineering

October 6, 2024 by Amoye Walters

During my time at university, I have learned an incredible amount, especially considering that Software Engineering was a completely new field for me. There were certainly rough times, but I am grateful for the decision to venture into this area despite having little to no prior knowledge. This journey has been challenging yet rewarding, and it has sparked a genuine interest and passion for the field. Throughout my studies, I am hoping to work on various projects that will significantly enhance my understanding and skills. From developing small applications to collaborating on larger, more complex systems, I look forward to each experience contributing to my growth as a software engineer. These projects will not only improve my technical abilities but also teach me the importance of teamwork, problem-solving, and continuous learning.

As I approach the completion of my program, I am hopeful that the skills and knowledge I have gained will equip me to perform to the best of my abilities. I aim to serve my future employers and colleagues with dedication and competence, contributing meaningfully to any team I join. I am excited about the future and look forward to the opportunities and challenges that lie ahead in my software engineering career.
